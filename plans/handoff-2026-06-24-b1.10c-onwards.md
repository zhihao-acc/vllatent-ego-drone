## 6. Candidate Hypotheses - Do Not Treat As Decisions Yet

These are prompts for investigation, not approved changes. After run2, the leading hypothesis is
objective/parameterization mismatch, but the next session must still research and present the
evidence before implementation.

- **Persistence is too strong at 5 Hz / 4-step horizon.** The model may learn useful motion but
  raw DINOv3 latent cosine rewards "copy current frame" too much.
- **The 57M predictor is too large for ~22K windows.** The plan already says B-1 is
  architecture-validation and data-scale, not a deployable convergence regime.
- **The objective may need residual or delta prediction.** Predicting `z_{t+k} - z_t`, adding a
  residual path, or reporting motion-sensitive diagnostics might better test dynamics, but this
  requires plan discussion.
- **Action-FiLM may be noisy.** MegaSaM scale/drift is non-load-bearing for B-1 targets, but it
  may still degrade conditioning.
- **The validation fold may be high variance.** The plan called for rotating 2-3 folds; one fold
  with `ski03` in val may not be representative.
- **Numerics are not clean.** The late NaN under bf16/LR 2e-4/WD 0.05 needs a fix even though
  `ckpt_best` predates it.
- **Data quality or duplicates may dominate.** Source-level QC passed visually, but per-source
  latent/persistence diagnostics are still needed.

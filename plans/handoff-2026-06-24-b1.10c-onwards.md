## 0. What this project is (cold-start in 60 seconds)

`vllatent-ego-drone` (pkg `vllatent`, repo `/home/zh/CODE/vllatent-ego-drone`) — a **compact
latent world-action model for a sports-following (skiing-primary) drone**.

```
RGB 224² ─► [DINOv3 ViT-B/16, FROZEN, CACHED] ─► z_t (196×768 fp16)
                                   │  + action(4-DoF, FiLM) + dt(FiLM)

                                   │
                          [WaypointHead MLP D→256→128→4]  ◄── B-2 (DEFERRED, see §3)
```

## 1. The scope decision that defines this phase (read before doing anything)

**B-1 trains the LATENT PREDICTOR ONLY. The waypoint head is DEFERRED to Phase B-2a.**
**B-1 DoD = a good latent world model** — the predictor must **beat a persistence baseline**
(`cos(z_t, z_{t+k})`, "next frame ≈ current frame") by a clear per-horizon margin on real held-out
val. Two head-side issues are unresolved and own B-2a: (1) **MegaSaM scale inconsistency** (`L_wp`'s
target is the monocular-VO delta, scale per-clip ambiguous/drifting), (2) **prober decision** (MLP
vs SkyJEPA PI-Prober vs attentive-pool). The predictor trains on **clean GT DINOv3 latents** (no
scale ambiguity) = the well-posed half. Do **not** train the waypoint head in B-1.

`reports/training-policy-research-2026-06-25.md` is **REFERENCE ONLY** — only its staged *schedule*
was salvaged; its PI-Prober/AdaLN/visual-bottleneck/SkyJEPA/GRPO/SIGReg are rejected (violate the
lean-architecture + no-anti-collapse locks). Do not implement them.
